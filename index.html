About Us

We believe urban mobility should be fluid, sustainable, and fun. Our mission is to provide easily accessible electric kickbikes to help you navigate your city with ease.

Our Story

Founded in 2023 by city dwellers frustrated with traffic, Fluid Motion started with a simple idea: make getting around town effortless. We deployed our first fleet in San Francisco and have been growing ever since.

Our Values

  • **Sustainability:** Committed to eco-friendly transportation.
  • **Accessibility:** Making mobility easy and affordable.
  • **Innovation:** Constantly improving our tech and user experience.
  • **Community:** Engaging locally to enhance urban life.

How To Use

1. Find a Bike

Tap 'Bikes' in the bottom dock to see available kickbikes.

2. Book & Pay

Select 'Book'. Confirm the initial charge, then tap 'Pay & Unlock'.

3. Ride Safely

Kick off gently. Wear a helmet, obey traffic laws, be aware.

4. Park & End Ride

Park upright in a designated zone. Tap 'End Ride' in the active ride modal.
